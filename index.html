⚙️ How it works

Three simple steps to
zero food waste

PlateZero makes sustainable eating effortless — for both students and mess administrators.

Step 01
📷
Scan & Attend
Students scan the mess QR code at entry to mark attendance. No manual registers, no queues — instant and contactless.
Step 02
🍽️
Snap Your Clean Plate
After finishing a meal with zero waste, snap a photo. Our system verifies and rewards you instantly with EcoPoints.
Step 03
🎁
Earn & Redeem
Climb the leaderboard, build streaks, and redeem EcoPoints for real rewards — ice cream, coffee, certificates and more.

🌍 The Problem

Food waste is a crisis hiding in plain sight

Universities generate massive food waste — not from malice, but because existing systems give students no reason to care. PlateZero changes that.

  • 🍚
    Students over-serve themselves with no accountability or visibility into waste generated
  • 💸
    Mess administrators spend thousands monthly on food that ends up in the bin
  • 🌱

  • 📉
    Admins lack real-time data to make smart decisions on portion sizes and menu planning
