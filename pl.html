The Problem We're Solving

Food waste is a major issue in universities, with students often over-ordering or leaving uneaten food. PlateZero helps reduce this waste by encouraging responsible eating habits and providing valuable data to administrators.Most of this waste is not intentional; it happens because existing systems do not encourage mindful consumption. PlateZero changes that by creating a culture of sustainability through positive reinforcement.
